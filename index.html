FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the project assignment work if I'm busy with college?

Each project has a flexible deadline (usually 1-3 weeks). You can choose projects that fit your college schedule, and there is no limit to the number of projects you can take simultaneously — it all depends on your own capacity.

Are these projects paid or unpaid?

The majority of projects offer direct compensation from business partners. Some projects are voluntary with rewards in the form of certificates and professional endorsements. Compensation status is always clearly stated in each project brief.

What happens if my work is rejected by a mentor?

The mentor will provide detailed feedback along with points that need improvement. You have the opportunity for up to 2 revisions. The goal is not to bring you down, but to ensure the quality of your output matches industry standards.

How long does the verification process take?

Once you submit a deliverable, the mentor will review it within a maximum of 2x24 working hours. After passing verification, certificates and badges are automatically issued to your profile.

Do I need to be a student to sign up?

Avyra is open to active students, fresh graduates (max. 2 years post-graduation), and career switchers. As long as you want to prove your skills through real projects, you qualify.
