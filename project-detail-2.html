📋 Project Description

HealthyBoost is planning to expand its operations from Jakarta to three new cities: Bandung, Surabaya, and Semarang. We need a thorough market research to understand the local demand and competitor landscape.

This project is perfect for economics or business students who want to apply their research and analytical skills to a real-world expansion strategy.

📦 Deliverables

  • ✓
    Competitor Matrix: Analysis of at least 5 local healthy drink competitors in each target city.
  • ✓
    Customer Persona: Identification of primary and secondary target demographics.
  • ✓
    Pricing Strategy: Recommended price points based on local market affordability.
